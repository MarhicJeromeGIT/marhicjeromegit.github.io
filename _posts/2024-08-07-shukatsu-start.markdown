The writing had been on the wall for a while now, but the recent 5 days a week RTO (Return To Office) mandate was the final reason I needed to start looking for a new job. Mixed feelings about it, I was pretty comfy after almost 5 years at the same company but it might prove a good thing eventually to shake things up a bit and go back on the job market.
Not going into details, but in the past month I've felt like I've outgrown the company a bit and that makes the change easier. Not that it's a guarantee the next job will be better, but with more experience I know what to look for and what to expect. If only I could get a SCRUMless team, I might even accept a 10% salary reduction !

OK so far I've been doing a bit of Leetcode exercises, I've registered a new domain name ([jmarhic.com](https://www.jmarhic.com)), I've setup my github page and refreshed my resume and LinkedIn profile ! Enough procrastination, the next step is to start sending applications for real.
